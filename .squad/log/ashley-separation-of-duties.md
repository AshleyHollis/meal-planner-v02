# Decision: Separation of Duties for Implementation Review

## Authority
Ashley Hollis (2026-03-09 current session)

## Decision
Testing and code review should not be performed by the same person who implemented the feature. Reviewer structure under review to establish clear separation between implementer and reviewer roles.

## Rationale
Quality assurance requires independent verification. The implementer has inherent knowledge and assumptions about their work that can blind them to issues an independent reviewer would catch. Separating testing and review from implementation strengthens team accountability and improves defect detection.

## Scope
- Applies to all future squad tasks and milestone work
- Reviewer role assignment and conflict resolution to be defined in subsequent meeting
- Does not retroactively change completed milestones 1–3

## Status
Under review; squad/reviewer structure (role clarity, assignment mechanics, and escalation paths) to be clarified in next planning session.
